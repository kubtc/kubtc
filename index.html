Step 1: Create a Wallet

Download Trust Wallet to create a wallet. Remember the seed phrase and NEVER share it with anyone! It's basically your password. Then copy the contract address below.

2

Step 2: Add Fugazi to your Wallet

Tap the icon on the top-right corner and search for "Fugazi". If it's not there, scroll all the way down and tap "Add Custom Token".

For Network, set BNB to "Smart Chain". Now copy the contract address from this website above and paste it in the Contract Address box.

It should autofill "Fugazi" for the name, "FUG" for the symbol, and "18" for the decimals.

Click "Save" and you should now have Fugazi added to your wallet!

3

Step 3: Buy "Binance Smart Chain" (BSC)

On the "Wallet" tab of the Trust Wallet app, tap "Smart Chain" and then "Buy" on the top right corner. Go through the steps to purchase Smart Chain coins. You may have to verify your identity.

4

Step 4: Swap BSC for Fugazi!

Once you have BSC in your Trust Wallet, go to the DApps (or "Browser" for iPhones) section from the bottom menu. If the Browser button is not visible at the bottom for iPhone, open Safari and in the address bar, type trust://browser_enable, then return to Trust Wallet.

Now open the "DApps" or "Browser" section. Find and open PancakeSwap. Tap "Connect" to connect your Trust Wallet. Scroll down to the "Exchange" box.

Click the icon and set the slippage to 12%. Make sure it is set to V2 and not V1.

Input the amount of Smart Chain BNB you want to swap for Fugazi, click Swap, and wait for the transaction to complete. This process is not always instant and can take few minutes sometimes if the network is congested.

Now you're officially a Fugazi holder!

Troubleshooting Tips

You may run into problems swapping if you didn't leave some Smart Chain BNB leftover to use as gas (the app uses BNB as gas). Make sure the slippage is set to 12%. Lastly, you may have to manually change the amount of Fugazis in the swap box to end with the number "1" instead of "0".
